Colour In-Line Game

Game Instructions

In this game can take a part two players. Each player has their color (blue or pink). Players take turns putting their colors in empty squares. The first player to get 4 cells of their color in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner. If all 16 squares are full and no player collects 4 cells of their color in a line, the game ends in a tie.
